# notaco
A local-only time tracking app where entries cannot be edited — only added or deleted. Built for self-discipline, inspired by the internet slang "TACO" (talk straight, mean what you say). No sign‑up, no database, no user accounts.
Notaco is a time logging app designed to help you stay disciplined and accountable — with no cheating.

The name comes from the internet catchphrase “TACO” (meaning “talk straight and act on it”), transformed into Notaco — because you say what you'll do, and the record cannot be changed later.

✅ No editing allowed — once a time entry is added, it stays as-is.

✅ Add or delete entries (no modification in between).

✅ Works entirely offline — all data is saved in your browser's local storage.

✅ No user accounts, no sign‑up, no backend database.

✅ Pure local-first design — your data never leaves your device.

Perfect for anyone who wants to track their time honestly, without the temptation to rewrite history.
